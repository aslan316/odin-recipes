Gochujang Gnocchi

If you're looking for a good, savory, and short-to-put-together recipe to make, this is for you!

  • Gnocchi
  • Gochujang chili paste
  • Tomato paste
  • Cream
  • Minced garlic
  • Diced shallot
  • Pecorino romano cheese
  1. Saute diced shallot in a skillet until translucent
  2. Add diced garlic and cook until golden brown
  3. Add equal parts gochujang and tomato paste and saute until a rusty color
  4. Add cream and bring to a low boil
  5. Add gnocchi and pecorino romano
  6. Once cream sauce has reduce and gnocchi has softened, serve hot!

A delicious recipe that is great for date nights! Gnocchi always has a classy appeal that is sure to impress anyone.
