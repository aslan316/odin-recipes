Brownies

If you want the fudgiest and chocolatiest brownies you've ever had, this recipe is just the thing for you

  • 2 eggs
  • Powdered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract
  1. Combine the sugar, flour, powdered sugar, cocoa powder, chocolate chips, and salt in a medium bowl
  2. Whisk together the eggs, olive oil, and water in a large bowl
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients in the larger of the two bowls
  4. Line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper
  5. Pour batter in pan
  6. Bake in a 325-degree over for 40-45 minutes
  7. Let cool, and then cut and serve!

These sweet treats are delicious and perfect to be fridgerated and saved for a later date! Enjoy!
