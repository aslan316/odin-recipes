Cajun Pasta

If you're looking for an easy, semi-spicy, highly-delcious chicken recipe, this one is for you!

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choosing
  • Chicken
  • Cream
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Roasted red peppers
  • Spinach
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Paprika
  • Ground mustard seed
  • White pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Pecorino remano cheese

Instructions

  1. Cooked cubes and marinated chicken in 1tbs of oil
  2. Remove cooked chicken from pan
  3. In same pan, saute onions in chicken juices until translucent
  4. Add minced garlic and saute until golden brown
  5. Add spincach and saute
  6. Add some of the cajun seasoning and the roasted red peppers
  7. Add pasta and stir as to gain flavor
  8. Quickly add cream and rest of seasonings
  9. Let cream come to a boil, then add lemon juice and some pecorino romano
  10. Cook until cream reduces into a thick sauce

This meal reheats extremely well, and is the perfect family meal for any tired adult
